Connecting Devices

Network Interface Card(NIC)

  • Enables the device to exchange data through a network
  • Installed inside a network device, such as computer or router
  • Most of it are connected to the motherboard of a computer through peripheral computer interconnect slot
  • Some uses USB connection such as wireless NICs which improves signal reception

Hub and Switch


A hub serves as a connection point of different devices in the same lan. Data entering a hub is fowared to all devices connected to it.

A switch connects different devices within the same LAN and direct data to targeted devices based on their MAC address.

Bridge


Similar to a switch, but it connects fewer LANs than the latter.


Router


Link between LANs or a LAN and a WAN and finds the shortest path to the destination for data packets.
